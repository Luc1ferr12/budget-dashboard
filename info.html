Dashboard

Get a quick and beautiful overview of your finances! Instantly see your income, savings, expenses, and balance in one glance. Explore interactive charts and filter your cash flow by period, month, or year. Your financial health, visualized.

  • 💰 Income: Shows the total money you have earned.
  • 🏦 Savings: Displays the total amount you have saved or transferred to savings/investments.
  • 💸 Expenses: Shows the total money you have spent.
  • 🧮 Balance: Your current available balance after all transactions.
💸

Transactions

Easily manage all your financial activities. Add, edit, or delete income, expenses, savings, and transfers. Enjoy clear tables, smart summaries, and a seamless experience to keep your records organized and up to date.

  • ➕ Add Saving: Use this to save money and move it to another bank account or investment account. This action is recorded as a saving and will be reflected in the Savings card on the Dashboard.
  • 🔄 Transfer Balance: Use this to transfer balance between your bank or investment accounts. The transferred amount will not affect the Savings card on the Dashboard; it is just a balance move.
  • 💵 Add Income: Record new income transactions, such as salary or other earnings, to increase your balance.
  • 💳 Add Expenses: Record your spending or payments to track and manage your expenses.
  • ✏️ Edit: Click the blue pencil button in the Actions column to modify an existing transaction.
  • 🗑️ Delete: Click the red trash button in the Actions column to remove a transaction permanently.
